All findings are based on: (a) information provided and reviewed; (b) information that was missing, incomplete, or inconsistent — recorded as a finding; and (c) direct observations made during the site visit. Inala does not independently verify the authenticity of documents received.

Engagement protocol

From first contact
to final report.

No surprises. Every step confirmed in writing before it begins. The entire engagement runs across exactly two channels — WhatsApp for buyer communication, Google Drive for document exchange with the seller side.

Phase 1+2 report: 5 working days after site visit
Phase 3 report: 5 working days after checkpoint
01
Initiation via WhatsApp
Send "Request Due Diligence" to +27 63 714 8225. Initial conversation to understand your transaction and key risk areas. No fee at this stage.
02
Engagement letter signed
Scope, fee, timeline, travel estimate, and all four mandatory notices confirmed in writing. Deposit invoice issued. No work begins until deposit is cleared.
03
Document request + questionnaire to Drive
Document list and pre-interview questionnaire added to a protected Google Drive folder. Access granted to the seller and/or their agreed-upon representative for uploading. Questionnaire due back at least 2 working days before the Phase 1 call.
04
Phase 1 · Baseline Trace (3–6 months)
Interview conducted with the seller and/or their agreed-upon representative, guided by questionnaire responses. Inala reviews the 3–6 month records on Drive. If this period cannot be traced or replicated, that is a documented finding. Risk hypothesis produced.
05
Phase 2 · Site Validation
Single structured site visit. Seller and/or their agreed-upon representative must be present. 10-point non-negotiables checklist completed in real time. Phase 1+2 report delivered within 5 working days via WhatsApp.
◉
Checkpoint Meeting Decision gate
You have the discovery report and site findings. You decide whether Phase 3 adds value. If you stop here: engagement closes. No further fee. No further obligation. If you proceed: Phase 3 addendum signed and deposit cleared before work begins.
06
Phase 3 · Financial Triangulation (if confirmed)
Full 12-month records already on Drive from the initial request — no going back to the seller. Valuation range and investment recommendation delivered within 5 working days of checkpoint via WhatsApp.
07
Report delivered to buyer via WhatsApp
Final report shared directly with you on WhatsApp. Two channels only: WhatsApp for all buyer communication, Google Drive for all document exchange with the seller side.
