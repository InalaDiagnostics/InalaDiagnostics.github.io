Why analyst-written at this tier
Fellows are professionals selected through a competitive process. An automated templated output is immediately visible as such and would undermine the prestige of the fellowship. Every Curriculum Signal Output at this tier is written by an Inala analyst — pattern statement, flag text, first expansion move — specific to this fellow and their business. This is what distinguishes a fellowship instrument from a survey.
Fellowship ROI framing: Corporate sponsors recover measurably more value per fellowship hour when fellows begin with a structural map of their own capacity. The Score Shift Report is the before-and-after structural measure that attendance data and completion certificates cannot produce — designed to slot directly into ESG and sustainability reporting frameworks without additional data processing.
